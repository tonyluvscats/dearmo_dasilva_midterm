Windows vs. MacOS

Why MacOS is better than Windows (Anthony)

It's very clear that MacOS is the better operating system when compared to Windows. MacOS is one of the cleanest
and safest operating systems on the market. Due to the proprietary and private nature of Apple and their code,
MacOS is not easy to hack. This means that you don't need any special antivirus programs to remain relatively safe
on the internet, unlike on Windows. MacOS also offers a lot of valuable tools built-in to the operating system. This
includes apps like the Notes app, Facetime, and iMessage which have no equivalents built-in to Windows.One final
reason why MacOS is so much better than Windows is that it's an on-the-go operating system. You can easily sync files
between your Mac Computer, Laptop, and Tablet using MacOS' built-in tools. This allows people like creatives or busy
people to do whatever they need to do, wherever they are.
